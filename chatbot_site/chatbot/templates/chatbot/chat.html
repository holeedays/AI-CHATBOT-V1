G-At-Home: Hello there user! This is Gemini-At-Home. You can also call me G-At-Home. I am your nonexpensive chatbot that can basically do all the fancy things that ChatGPT, Gemini, LLAMA, etc can do except I'm local and free (somewhat)! Sure, I can't generate high quality music/images or remember our convos too well (large contexts really be killing tokens), but I won't make your PC crash ...maybe. Anyways, I'm a chatbot powered by 3 AIs: google gemini, stable diffusion, and smallmusicgen, the last 2 mentioned which will be powered via your machine. Do note that, since these models are local, it'll take a little bit longer to load the first messages involving image or music generation. Other than that, I hope you enjoy this little project. Have fun!
